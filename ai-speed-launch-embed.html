If You Get Stuck
"I don't know how to write a good AI prompt."
→ Screenshot your old design → upload to ChatGPT → ask for 10 style variations → pick one → ask for the full prompt.
"I'm trying to revive a listing that never sold."
→ Stop. Glow-ups are for winners. Pick something with real sales history.
"Should I rewrite the SEO?"
→ No. If it ranked before, leave it. Only change images and files.
