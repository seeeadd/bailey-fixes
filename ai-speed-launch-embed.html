Upgrade Title

5 min

Only touch if outdated. If it ranked before, leave it alone.

Tags

5 min

Keep tags that work. Add 1-2 new ones for your updated style.

STAGE 5

Pricing

2 min

A fresh design can justify a small price bump. Test it.

Check competitor pricing for similar items
Consider $0.50-1.00 increase for premium look
Update price in Vela
Test tip: Try higher pricing for 2 weeks. If sales drop, adjust back.
