STEP 3

Fix the Description

10 min

Your description needs to answer 4 questions. That's it.

What is it?

1-2 sentences: what it is and who it's for

What it's NOT (most people skip this!):

"This is a digital file only. No physical product will be shipped."
If true: "This is NOT an SVG and cannot be used with Cricut/Silhouette."

What's included?

Number of files, file types, sizes, resolution

How to use it?

Recommended software + equipment needed
Usage rights (personal + small commercial, no reselling file)
Refund policy ("Digital product â€” no refunds")
Break long walls of text into short paragraphs. Nobody reads blocks.
