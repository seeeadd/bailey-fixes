Prompts You'll Need

Step 1 — Get Style Variations
Give me 10 different style variation ideas to remake this design that would sell well on Etsy in the [NICHE] niche.
Step 2 — Get the Full Prompt
Write me a full AI art prompt for variation [NUMBER]. Make it [STYLE WORDS like "grungy" or "metallic" or "watercolor"]. Do not include the words "tumbler wrap design" — just say "digital design."
Step 3 — Make It More Unique
Rewrite this prompt to be more unique and trendy. Add [ELEMENT like "gold metallic effects" or "camouflage"]. Remove any reference to tumblers.
If You Get Stuck
"I don't know how to write a good AI prompt."
→ Screenshot your old design → upload to ChatGPT → ask for 10 style variations → pick one → ask for the full prompt. It does the work.
"I'm trying to revive a listing that never sold."
→ Stop. Glow-ups are for winners. Pick something with real sales history.
"Should I rewrite the SEO?"
→ No. If it ranked before, leave it. Only change images and files.
"The AI messed up the text."
→ Add text manually in Kittl. Font → type → stroke/shadow. 2 minutes.
Bailey's Rules for Glow-Ups
  1. 1

    Pick listings with consistent sales and high conversion — if people buy when they find it, demand exists.

  2. 2

    Cross-niche for broader appeal — Christian + military + men = 3 audiences.

  3. 3

    Men prefer realistic, women prefer graphic — adjust generator.

  4. 4

    Duplicate, don't edit from scratch — same SEO, new art.

  5. 5

    Check quantity in Vela — back to 999 after duplicating.
