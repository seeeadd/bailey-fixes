Workflow 2

Glow-Up

Upgrade one existing listing with a fresh AI design. Same SEO, new art, more sales.

The Short Version

You have listings that sold. They look dated now — old graphics, tired trends, non-AI designs from 2022. The demand is still there. The SEO already works. You duplicate the listing, swap in an upgraded design, and publish. That's it.

Where You'll Get Stuck

"I don't know how to write a good AI prompt."
Screenshot your old design → upload to ChatGPT → ask for 10 style variations → pick one → ask ChatGPT for the full prompt. It does the work.
"I'm trying to revive a listing that never sold."
Stop. Glow-ups are for winners, not losers. Pick something with real sales history.
"Should I rewrite the SEO?"
No. If it ranked before, leave it. Only change images and files.
"The AI messed up the text."
Add text manually in Kittl. Pick a font, type the words, add a stroke/shadow. Done in 2 minutes.
"Do I edit the original listing or make a new one?"
Bailey duplicates the listing and keeps the SEO. If you'd rather edit the original, that's fine — just don't change the keywords.

Prompts

Write me a full AI art prompt for variation [NUMBER]. Make it [STYLE WORDS like "grungy" or "metallic" or "watercolor"]. Do not include the words "tumbler wrap design" — just say "digital design."
Step 3 — Make It More Unique
Rewrite this prompt to be more unique and trendy. Add [ELEMENT like "gold metallic effects" or "camouflage"]. Remove any reference to tumblers.

Bailey's Rules for Glow-Ups

  1. 1

    Pick listings with consistent sales and high conversion — even if sales have slowed, if people still buy when they find it, the demand is there.

  2. 2

    Cross-niche for broader appeal — Christian + military + men = 3 audiences instead of 1.

  3. 3

    Men prefer realistic, women prefer graphic — adjust your generator choice accordingly.

  4. 4

    Duplicate, don't edit from scratch — same SEO, new art. That's it.

  5. 5

    Check your quantity in Vela — make sure it's back to 999 after duplicating.

What to Look For in Trend Research

Signal What It Means
Listing age Less than 3-4 months old (newer = trend is still hot)
Sales High number relative to age
Badges Bestseller or Popular Now
Social proof "6 people bought this in the last 24 hours"

If it has 2-3 of these, it's good enough. Stop researching and start creating.

Where You'll Get Stuck

"I've been scrolling for 20 minutes looking for the perfect trend."
Stop at 5 minutes. Pick something with: listing age <3-4 months + high sales + badge. That's enough. Move on.
"I don't know what tags to use."
Look at the listing you got inspiration from. Copy their keywords + think like a buyer. Done.
"I want to make 5 products."
Make 1. Ship it. Then make the next one.

Workflow 3

Niche Pack

Own a niche with multiple products + a bundle. One design concept, 10+ listings.

The Short Version

Some niches have built-in variations — awareness ribbons (10+ cancers), states (50), hobbies, countries. Instead of making one product and hoping, you make all the variations at once, list them individually, then bundle them together. Bailey's state designs bundle: $10,000+.

Pick one where you can make 10+ variations without inventing new concepts.

Where You'll Get Stuck

"I can't pick a niche."
Start with awareness ribbons, states, or hobbies. These always have built-in variations.
"I'm recreating SEO for each listing."
No. Duplicate your base listing. Use Find & Replace in Vela. Only change the niche-specific words. Everything else stays.
"One prompt came out bad."
Skip it. Don't let 1 bad prompt stall 9 good ones. You can redo it later.

Bailey's Rules for Niche Packs

  1. 1

    Start with awareness, states, or hobbies — these always have built-in variations. Don't overthink niche selection.

  2. 2

    Don't let 1 bad prompt stall 9 good ones — skip it, come back later.

  3. 3

    Use Find & Replace in Vela — only change the niche-specific words. Everything else stays.

  4. 4

    Price new listings low to rank faster — Bailey starts at $1 for a short window. Just don't leave it there forever — set a date or sales count where you raise it.

  5. 5

    Bundle delivery = PDF + Google Drive — Etsy's 5-slot / 20MB limit won't fit 10+ designs. Create a PDF in Canva with the Drive link.
